Some documents and wiki pages that have been used by Freegle over the years have been superseded or become redundant, but have been kept as an archive.

  • Most pages below have been deleted, so will not appear in general searches of the wiki. Wiki administrators can see the text of deleted pages.
  • The pages marked with an asterisk below have been copied and pasted onto googledocs for external reference.
    • For pages A-L, see https://docs.google.com/document/d/1WfWos02UN7VP2tKgwjjSRd5szQVl39f8-ZVpHZvlJf0/edit?usp=sharing

  • Pages that have been archived and still searchable in the wiki are in Category:Historic documents
  • External document links are at the bottom of the list below.
