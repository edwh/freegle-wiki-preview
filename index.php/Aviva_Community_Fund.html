2017 Competition

Timetable

  • 12th September 2017 - competition opened.
  • 10th October - closed for submissions.
  • 24th October - voting opened.
  • 21st November - voting closed at midday. We finished 6th in our project category.
  • 28th November - finalists announced.
  • 16th January 2018 - winners announced.
