2012

Sheffield Transition Awards 2012 - Reuse/Recycling category winner.

2009

Apply Yourself!

Have a look at your local council website to see if they run any community award schemes. Don't be shy about applying - your group is doing a great community service!

For other possible awards, have a look at this website - http://www.environmentawards.net/ - that gives a good list of environmental award opportunities.
