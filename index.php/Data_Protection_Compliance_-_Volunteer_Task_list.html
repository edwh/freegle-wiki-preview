Completed tasks

As of 22nd March 2018, the volunteer dealing with Data Protection compliance had completed the following:

Task Completion comments
Assign Data Protection Officer Although it isn't a legal or regulatory requirement for an organisation such as Freegle to have a DPO, we have a volunteer assigned to this role. There is also a generic email address for this function: DPO@ilovefreegle.org
Create Data Use & Protection Wiki page This has been created and is mostly complete.
Document Freegle's legal basis for data processing and national jurisdiction Complete - we have chosen to use "Legitimate Interest" as our legal basis, and have included a Legitimate Interest Assessment (LIA), as suggested by ICO guidance.
Create Data Protection Policy document This clearly spells out Freegle's policy on:
- Legal basis
- Subject Access Requests
- Notification of data breaches
- Children's accounts and guardian consent
- Design of data protection
- User-requested data deletion
- Data retention policy

The DPO drafted the initial policy and worked with system owners (Freegle Direct, Norfolk, Trash Nothing) to ensure clarity. March 2020 - Norfolk groups moved to Freegle Direct from their own platform.
Create Data Protection Guidance This explains how the policies are put into practice, for system owners and volunteers.
Remove Yahoo Group references Completed 4/12/18.
