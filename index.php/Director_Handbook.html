This Handbook is designed to introduce new Directors to Freegle and assist you in understanding your legal and regulatory responsibilities. It also serves as a resource and refresher for existing Trustees.

As a Director, you will be able to use your skills and experience to directly influence the governance, strategic objectives and to help carry out business for the benefit of the community. This Handbook also tells you what to expect from us and where to go for further advice and guidance should you need it. Welcome to Freegle.
