Freegle UK - Freegle Representative Group (Reps)

2012. Nominations for the 2012 annual election for Reps were requested by special notice on Freegle UK Central and by a mail to all group owner addresses on 20th November 2012. Candidate statements for the Reps Election 2012 and the Q&A session were notified on Central on 1st December. Summary of Q&A: [1]. The election notice was sent to all groups and posted on Central on 19th December 2012. The provisional results were announced on Central on 21st December 2012. The final result was announced on Central on 24th December 2012.

2011. Nominations for the 2011 annual election for Reps were requested by a mail to all group owner addresses along with a special notice on Central on 30th October 2011. Nominations were notified on Central on 7th November 2011. Candidate statements (Reps11-11) and the Q&A session were notified on Central on 13th November 2011. The election notice was sent to all groups and posted on Central on 25th November 2011. The provisional results were announced on Central on 6th December 2011. The Elected Roles Autopromotion procedure was used on 8th January 2012.

2010/2011. Nominations for the 2010 annual election for Reps were requested by a mail to all group owner addresses along with a special notice on Central on 19th December 2010. Nominations were notified on Central. Candidate statements and the Q&A session were notified on Central on 15th January 2011. The results were announced on Central on 16th February 2011.

2009. The election for the first team of Reps was announced on Central and sent to all group owner addresses on 12th December 2009. Nominations had been previously requested via Central on 1st December. The results were announced on Central on 24th December 2009.

Freegle UK Returning Officer

2013 (Nov). The election for a Returning Officer was announced on Central and sent to all group owner addresses on 3rd November 2013. Candidate statements (RO Election November 2013) and the Q&A session were notified on Central on 14th November 2013. The election notice was announced on Central on 20th November 2013. The result was announced on Central on 30th November 2013.

2013 (Jun). The election for a Returning Officer was announced on Central and sent to all group owner addresses on 10th June 2013. Candidate statements (RO Election June 2013) and the Q&A session were notified on Central on 20th June 2013. The election notice was sent to all group owner addresses on 29th/30th June 2013 and announced on Central. The provisional result was announced on Central on 9th July 2013, with the final announcement made on 10th July.

2012. The election for a Returning Officer was announced on Central and sent to all group owner addresses on 10th December 2011. Candidate statements (RO Election January 2012) and the Q&A session were notified on Central on 10th January 2012. The election notice was sent to all group owner addresses on 19th and 20th January 2012 and announced on Central. The result of the election was announced on Central on 31st January 2012.

2010. The election for a Returning Officer in 2010 was announced on Central and sent to all group owner addresses on 8th June 2010. The result of the election was announced on Central on 8th June 2010. A Deputy Returning Officer was appointed by the Returning Officer on 16th June 2010.

Freegle UK Ombudsman

2013. Nominees were requested for the two positions of Ombudsman on 21st July 2013. Appointments were announced on Central on 29th July 2013.

See Ombudsman Election 2013 for details.

2010. Nominees were requested for the position of Ombudsman on 21st June 2010. The result of the election was announced on Central on 22nd August 2010.
