Freegle Tech Report December 2018 Messages 26500-26693 Members 115

General

A quiet month as is usual for December. Edward spent Christmas tweaking stuff and catching up on his bucket list.
Google are killing off Google Plus, which we used in our Google sign-in code, so he had to change that. Let him know if you see anything odd with the Google signin.
Edward made some changes to the servers to use a CDN. This is a service which caches stuff from your web servers to make your site faster. https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Content_delivery_network.
We get access to the Azure CDN for free from Microsoft as part of their charity programme. This should improve the speed at which the FD loads. I'm not yet sure whether the difference will be that noticeable, but it's worth trying out.
Finally members are able to edit their posts and photos. There have been a few bugs with this, but it seems okay now.

Direct.

It was pointed out that the Settings page tells you how to leave individual groups, but it doesn't have a link to leave Freegle entirely. So we’ve added one.
A member complained he could not add a photo on his ‘My Posts’ just delete them so Edward added the option.
It was suggested that now members and Moderators have the option to edit posts, we may like to look at our auto mails. Instead of using delete options, groups could set up a couple where we edit them or we give members the link and suggest that they edit leaving them in hold for a couple of days.
It was suggested that being able to see how many members replied to an offer was counter-productive? Putting members off replying if they saw others had. It was discussed but there was no majority for change.

Q. These posts show they come for an IP address in Lithuania..
A.. It shows as Lithuania in other tools, e.g. https://www.whatismyip.com/ip-address-lookup/ as well. So either they're on holiday, or using an anonymisation tool.
Q. Is it possible that when editing a post the browser spell checker could be part of the process please? It works in Mod config messages I am sending but not if I am editing a pending message.

Mapping.

Q I was contacted by a member last night who was enquiring about an offer that hadn't appeared.I could see no trace of her offer, so I invited her to try again. She did - still no trace. Then, later this morning, I found two copies of her post... on Tendring Freegle. I told her, and she told me she had no idea how that could have happened as that was certainly not the intention.
A People used to join local groups on Yahoo depending on where they thought they belonged, I think it would be much preferable to allow people to pick a local group when they join via FD. How about a system where Freegle suggests nearby groups rather than pick a default.
Group boundaries are mapped when a group is started by Gat. Some members as this one did falls into a space between two. New members who post without joining a specific group are popped into the one which fits their location. This results in a lot less members having their first post removed, and told to join a different group. Gat are in the process of remapping that area.
Q. When I go to my explore groups and type in Westcliff or WOS my group does not come up, I have to type the full name.
A. Sorry it powered by Google and not in our control.
