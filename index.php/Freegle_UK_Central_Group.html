Team role and remit

The team undertakes a variety of tasks concerned with running our central Freegle discussion group. They work within guidelines outlined in Discourse Support Team Remit.

Moderation

To keep Central a friendly and supportive place for all volunteers we should:

  • state a minimum number of mods to be involved in the mod team (5) that do the work involved in running the group
  • run a regular survey to include feedback for why people have joined or left, why they would choose special notices, and whether the moderation in place is acceptable
  • apply a very light touch moderation process only, with the priority to keep Central as unmoderated as possible
  • only use moderation when messages are abusive, overtly aggressive or threatening

Moderation process:

  • the Central mods team have responsibility for moderation on the group
  • allow the Discourse Support Team to act in 'emergencies' to put a member on 'moderation' in line with their remit

Central guidelines

When posting it helps if you sign off with your name and your community name.

This is a self-moderated forum and as such we have no 'rules', but these are the guidelines we would like you to follow. By joining the group you agree to follow the guidelines below and agree that the Central Mod Team may moderate posts in extreme circumstances.

1. Please be polite and treat fellow members with respect.

2. If in doubt of another poster's tone, assume they are speaking neutrally. It is easier on your blood pressure.

3. Please do not use insulting, derogatory or obscene language toward fellow members.

4. Please do not forward in full or in part any correspondence unless the sender gives their consent.

Thank you very much. Please enjoy your stay in Freegle Central. In Freegle we always welcome feedback, and if you feel any of the above guidelines could be improved, or are being ignored, please feel free to e-mail the owners: centralmods@ilovefreegle.org

Best regards, Your Freegle Central Team.

Auto messages sent to members

Welcome message

Sent on approval of membership.

Welcome to the Freegle Central group, fellow Freegler :o)

Freegle Central is open to anyone who is a Freegle volunteer.

When posting it helps if you sign off with your name and your community name.

The purpose of Freegle Central is to provide a space for support of Freegle volunteers, so please ask if you have a question about anything.

This is a self-moderated forum and as such we have no 'rules', but these are the guidelines we would like you to follow.

Please be polite and treat fellow members with respect.

If in doubt of another poster's tone, assume they are speaking neutrally. It is easier on your blood pressure.

We ask you to respect that this is a private group - the information contained within this group is for Freegle volunteers only.

Thank you very much. Please enjoy your stay in Freegle Central! :)

In Freegle we always welcome feedback, and if you feel anything on Central could be improved, please feel free to e-mail us and one of our Mod team will reply to your feedback.

Best regards, Discourse Support Team

Pending member message

Thanks for your application to join Freegle Central.

This group is open to Freegle volunteers, and to verify your status, please can you arrange for verification from your owner address, or ask someone who is already a member of Central to email us with your details.

If you are a new group, we should have received this info from GAT, and/or a GAT volunteer will help with this.

We usually aim to approve applications within 24 hours of receiving confirmation.

If you want to start a new community - which would be great - then the place to start is newgroups@iLoveFreegle.org.

Kindest regards, Discourse Support Team

Email us if you require further information: centralmods@ilovefreegle.org

Rejection of membership message

This message is sent if there is no response from an applicant.

Thanks for applying to join Freegle Central. Unfortunately we were not able to verify that you are an owner or moderator of an affiliated Freegle community. For this reason your application has been rejected.

Please feel free to reapply and look out for the verification information we send if you feel this was an error, and please state your Freegle community name when you reapply.

If you are waiting for your community to be affiliated, please reapply when the affiliation process is completed.

If you are just looking for info about Freegle, please visit www.ilovefreegle.org

Kind regards,

Discourse Support Team
