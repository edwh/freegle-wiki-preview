Here's a variety of PR materials and resources you can use for your group:

  • Badges - links/logos to use on your home page and elsewhere.
  • Banner - borrow a Freegle banner, or print your own paper one; find out how here.
  • Business Cards - ideas for using business cards to promote your group, and Sending Business Cards for hints on economical postage.
  • Firefox Freegle Persona - uploading this gives your screen a Freegle 'skin' if you use Firefox.
  • Logos - Freegle logo graphics for webpages and printed materials.
  • Posters, fliers, cards - designs you can print yourself.
  • Presentations - sample PowerPoint presentations.
  • QR Codes
  • Shortlinks - lists and explanations of shortlinks to make your publicity easier.
  • Using the Logo - permission details for using the Freegle logo, plus font information.
  • https://www.ilovefreegle.org/app-store-black-sm.png - Apple App Store icon.
  • https://www.ilovefreegle.org/en-play-badge.png - Google Play icon.

If you have PR materials, good stories, or a report of a successful promotion you'd be happy to share with other groups, they'd be most welcome. Post a message on one of the Central Groups or Development Working Group, or email media@ilovefreegle.org.

If you'd like some custom graphics for your group, contact the Graphics Team.

Members can download publicity materials themselves at https://www.ilovefreegle.org/promote

Good luck with your local campaign!
