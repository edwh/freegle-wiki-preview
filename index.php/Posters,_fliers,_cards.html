Posters

https://www.ilovefreegle.org/posters/A4.jpg is a poster you can download and print, or send to your members to download and print themselves.

In April 2019, members were asked, via a centrally generated Admin message, to put up this poster in their area and give feedback on where they'd displayed it. A map shows the results: https://www.ilovefreegle.org/posters

Customised posters for each group

The Geeks have produced customised posters for every group. Download yours from Customised posters.

Having a clear out?

File:HavingAClearOut thumb.png File:HavingAClearOut 2019 generic.docx

Load this into Word and add your group's address in the appropriate place, then print it out and put it up in libraries, cafes, estate agents and so on. Make sure you ask for permission first.

Can't move for the clutter?

File:CantMoveForTheClutter thumb.png File:CantMoveForClutter2019generic.docx

Load this into Word and add your group's address in the appropriate place, then print it out and put it up as above.
