Here's a whole bunch of pages that might help you promote your group. Good luck! And remember, we'd love to hear what you've got up to, so please share - other groups love hearing about your stories and successes, so post messages on the Central Groups to spread the news.

OED definition: 'Promotion - activity that supports or encourages a cause, venture, or aim.'

Local Promotion Volunteers

Having a member of your group's volunteer team 'doing' promotional work - online, contacting people, events - is a good idea. Have a look at Group Promotion Volunteers for an outline of the work involved, and Recruiting, Training and Retaining for ideas on how to find someone to do it.
