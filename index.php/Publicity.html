Welcome to the Publicity Handbook

This handbook gathers the ideas and resources that help you raise your group's profile, grow its membership, and grow Freegle. See Category:Promotion & Publicity for every page in it.

Development Working Group is the Freegle working group covering all things publicity - all volunteers are welcome to join and share ideas.

Getting started

  • Promotion - the main hub of promotion ideas, from contacting your council to social media and PR materials. It also links to a spreadsheet of useful addresses where you might get your group mentioned.
  • Planning a Campaign - work out your message, goals and audience before a publicity push.
  • Promoting my new group - a simple checklist of what to do if you're just starting out.

Press and PR

  • Getting Press Coverage - how to get your story into the local press (there's a short version too).
  • Sample Press Release - a ready-to-adapt press release template.
  • Live Interviews - a crib sheet for preparing for a live interview.
  • Quote Bank - facts and quotes for emails, letters, appeals and posts.
  • Photo call - a sample photo-call notice.

Events and reuse

  • Running a local event? See Give and Take and Events.
  • National Reuse Day - Freegle's annual day celebrating and promoting reuse.
  • Meet volunteers from around the UK at the annual Freestock get-together.
