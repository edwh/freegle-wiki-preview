Sample messages to send to individual members, for many (if not all) occasions. Remember that your community's rules are your own - not necessarily the same as other Freegle communities - and that members appreciate a personal, friendly approach, not a corporate memo.

If you have ideas for improving these messages, or want to share some of your own, add them, or post on the Central group asking someone else to add them.

For messages you use often, add them to your Standard Messages for your community, and share them with the rest of your team.
